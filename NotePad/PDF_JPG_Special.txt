PDF (with multiple pages) to JPG (one jpg file)
gitbash
$ magick -density 200 cheat_sheet.pdf -background white -alpha remove -alpha off -quality 90 -append -resize 920x cheatSheet.jpg

Explanation:
----------------
-background white → sets the background color.

-alpha remove -alpha off → removes transparency (so no black fill).

-density 300 → keeps text crisp.

-quality 100 → best JPEG quality.

-append → all pages in one image.
